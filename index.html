Steve's Timeline

  • February 24, 1955: Steven Paul Jobs is born in San Francisco, California, USA, to Joanne Schieble and Abdulfattah Jandali. He is adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs.

  • 1969:Steve Jobs meets Steve Wozniak, a fellow electronics enthusiast and future co-founder of Apple Inc., while attending Homestead High School.

  • 1972:obs graduates from high school and enrolls at Reed College in Portland, Oregon. However, he drops out after just one semester.

  • 1974: Steve Jobs returns to California and starts attending meetings of the Homebrew Computer Club, where he becomes interested in the potential of personal computers.

  • April 1, 1976:Apple Computer, Inc. is founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in the Jobs family garage. They release the Apple I computer.

  • 1986:Steve Jobs acquires Pixar, a computer graphics division of Lucasfilm, and transforms it into a successfull animation studio.

  • October 5, 2011:Steve Jobs passes away at the age of 56 in Palo Alto, California, after a long battle with pancreatic neuroendocrine tumor.
